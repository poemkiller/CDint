How to Make a Cake

This recipe will show you how to make your own cake. Follow Closely.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/4 Cups Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 Vegtable Oil
  • Any Flavor icing
  • One teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 3/4 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 2 teaspoons of Baking Soda
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups Unsalted Butter

      • Instructions

      1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Grease three 9-inch cake pans, line with parchment paper, then grease the parchment paper.
      2. Begin by mixing together 2 cups of flour and sugar together in a bowl
      3. Add eggs, oil, and brown sugar into the mixture and mix until smooth
      4. Using a handheld or stand mixer fitted with a paddle or whisk attachment, beat the butter and sugar together on high speed until smooth and creamy, about 3 minutes
      5. Pour batter evenly into cake pans. Weigh them to ensure accuracy, if desired. Bake for around 23-26 minutes or until the cakes are baked through. To test for doneness, insert a toothpick into the center of the cake.
      6. Take out the cake and let it cool for 1-2 hours.
      7. When the cake is completely cool, evenly spread the icing all over the cake with a spreading knife.
